Traits
Blood Hex of the Direlord. Azoriandas is bound by a blood oath to kill Tiamat. If she is killed by anyone else or if he passes up an opportunity to kill her, he dies. Tiamat is considered hexed by Azoriandas at all times. Azoriandas gains the following bonuses against creatures he has hexed:
  • A hexed creature cannot use Legendary Resistance or Indomitable against an effect Azoriandas triggers
  • A Hexed creature has disadvantage on attack rolls against Azoriandas
  • ⚔ When Azoriandas hits a hexed creature, it cannot regain hitpoints until the start of his next turn
  • ⚔ Azoriandas crits on attack rolls of 18-20 when attacking a hexed creature
  • ⚔ Melee Attacks against a hexed creature deal 4d6 bonus damage
  • ⚔ When Azoriandas damages a hexed creature, it triggers his Bolstered Fury ability
  • Azoriandas has resistance to all damage dealt by a hexed creature
  • Azoriandas cannot be frightened by a creature he has hexed
Amphibious. Azoriandas can breathe air and water.
Bolstered Fury. When Azoriandas scores a critical hit or damages a hexed creature, he gains 40 temp HP. While he has 1 or more of these temp HP, any creature that damages him takes 20 necrotic damage.
Bonus Actions
(B) Cloud Leap. As a bonus action, Azoriandas can leap up to 120 in any direction.
Reactions & Between Turns
(R) Legendary Counter. If a creature uses a legendary action to make an attack roll against Azoriandas or that would force him to make a saving throw, he may immediately use his reaction to move up to his movement speed, provoking no opportunity attacks. If the action was an attack roll or saving throw based on an area of effect and he is no longer in the area, he avoids the effect. If the effect is sight based, he avoids it only if he is no longer within sight. If he successfully is no longer targeted by the legendary action, he can use Direbeam directed at the creature that used the legendary action.
Shards of Opportunity. If a creature provokes an attack of opportunity (20ft. reach), Azoriandas can choose to automatically make an attack without using a reaction as a shard of the blade flies from its place to strike the target before returning to its place. +19, 2d6+12 force
Indomitable (3 uses). If Azoriandas fails a saving throw, he can choose to succeed instead.
Magic Resistance. Azoriandas has advantage on saving throws against spells and other magical effects.
Action Surge. Take an additional action.
Actions
Multiattack. Azoriandas makes three attacks from the options below. If he damages a creature he has hexed, he uses his Bolstered Fury:
  • The Unsettled Shards (+3). 20ft. +19, 8d6+12 force (+4d6 bonus damage on hexed creatures)
  • Stomp. 5ft., one huge or smaller creature. +16, 5d10+9 bludgeoning. The target must succeed on a DC22 Dex save or have the prone condition. Until Azoriandas moves or uses Stomp again, the creature has the restrained condition. The restrained creature or another creature within 5ft can use an action to make a DC22 Strength check, on a success they relocate to an unoccupied space within 5 feet of Azoriandas and end the restrained condition.
(A) Direbeam (Recharge 5-6). 500ft., 10ft radius, DC22 dex/half 12d8 force, on failure stunned until end of next turn. The shards of the diresword spin out of place and swirl around as a black cloud gathers at the base, drawing the pieces inward. As they unite in the center, a dark beam of purple energy ripples streaks out forming a laser that focuses for a second before sputtering out, leaving the shards to snap back into place amidst a cloud of sulphuric smelling smoke.
(A) Shard Scatter (Recharge 5-6). 120/350 6 targets +18, 4d12+11 force, DC22 Con save or hexed until end of Azoriandas’s next turn.
